College is Stressful

Although, college can be depicted in movies as "the best time in your life," I would say that college is quite stressful especially in current times.

We have a lot on our plate as college students, especially because right now we are in the middle of a pandemic.

I am sure some of us are worried about finding a job after graduation, since things are very uncertain right now.

Some of us need a job right after college because we have loans to pay off!

This website will discuss the American College System, it's flaws, and explain why time management is such an important skill for college students.
