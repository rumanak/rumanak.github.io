An Overview of the College Education in America

The Promise

Many students go to college with the promise that it will help them land a better job than those who do not obtain a college degree.

The Cost

College is so expensive, that many students and their families will go into debt.

The Result

Although, many students every year make the decision to enroll in the higher education system, the value of a college degree is actually going down. This is because there is a growing number of jobs that don't require a degree.
