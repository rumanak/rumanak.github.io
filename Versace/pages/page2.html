This iconic collection was very controversial. People either hated it! Or they loved it! Some people thought it made women seem like sex-objects, which was not Versace's intention at all!

1994 Spring Collection

This 1994 collection is the collection in which the killer gold safety-pin dress was born in! It was familiar to Fsll 1992 Collection. This is remembered as Gianna Versace's most punk collection.
